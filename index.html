Employment Challenges for MBA Graduates

A striking 93% of MBA graduates in India are deemed unemployable due to a lack of requisite skills, particularly from Tier 2 and Tier 3 colleges. This highlights the importance of skill-based training and practical exposure, which your institute can offer to bridge this gap.

Accountant Demand in Europe

Europe faces a growing shortage of accountants, with over 2 million vacancies predicted as businesses continue to expand, creating lucrative opportunities for well-trained professionals.

BBA and BCom Graduates' Struggles

Many graduates from general business programs, such as BBA & BCom, lack the advanced skills demanded by employers, leading to underemployment. Institutes offering specialized courses & skill-building workshops, like yours, can help students stand out.

Demand for Higher Education with Specialization

The UK has a significant demand for professionals skilled in specific domains, such as accounting and business management tailored to local regulations. Offering focused programs in UK tax laws or European business strategies can provide students with an edge.

Global Competitiveness of Indian Talent

Indian management graduates with enhanced skill sets are increasingly in demand worldwide but lack practical experience. Programs that emphasize real-world projects, internships, and industry tie-ups have been shown to boost employment rates significantly.
